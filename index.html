Frequenty Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our movie information platform.

What is Cineast?

Cineast is an online movie and TV database where users can explore film details, cast information, ratings, reviews, and streaming availability — all in one place.

How can I rate or review a movie?

Simply sign up for a free Cineast account, visit the movie’s page, and click the “Rate & Review” button. You can leave your rating from 1–10 and write your personal review.

Does Cineast show where to stream movies?

Yes! Each movie or show page includes a “Where to Watch” section that lists streaming platforms where the title is currently available.

Can I create my own watchlist?

Absolutely. Cineast lets you create custom watchlists — just click “Add to Watchlist” on any title to save it for later viewing.

Is Cineast free to use?

Yes, Cineast is free for all users. However, we also offer Cineast Pro, a premium subscription with ad-free browsing, advanced analytics, and early access to new features.
